About National Statistical Office

The National Statistical Office operates under the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI). It is the principal agency responsible for large‑scale sample surveys, statistical data collection and dissemination of official statistics in India.

The Surat regional office manages field survey operations, training of investigators, data validation, and coordination of national statistical programs across the region.

Reliable statistics are essential for planning, economic development, welfare programs, and monitoring national progress.

Major Surveys Conducted

Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS)

Measures employment, unemployment, labour force participation and workforce statistics across India.

Household Consumption Expenditure Survey

Collects data on household consumption patterns used to estimate poverty and economic welfare.

Annual Survey of Industries

Provides detailed information about industrial production, employment and economic growth.

Socio‑Economic Surveys

Various national surveys that help government understand social and economic conditions.

Public Information
